Mallorca

~
An island of contrasts

Mallorca’s unique selling point must surely be its diversity. From luxury hotels to mountain refuges, local markets to upmarket fashion stores, and from tourist resorts to peaceful mountain footpaths, it truly is an island of contrasts.In the summer time it can be very hot and humid while one of the nicest times is Autumn when the tourist masses are gone, the temperatures cool down but it is still warm enough to enjoy outdoors. The weather on Mallorca is a major attraction for the millions of tourist that visit the island annually. With more than 300 days of sunshine, the clear, blue skies year round gives a very positive feeling even in winter time.
